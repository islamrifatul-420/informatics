My First 46 km Ride — Sylhet Tea Garden

Back in Bangladesh, I went on my first long 46 km ride with friends. We headed toward the Sylhet tea gardens through quiet morning roads. That ride became the moment I truly fell in love with cycling.

Ikebukuro → Mizuhodai — My First Long Ride in Japan

Only three days after arriving in Japan, I started exploring the roads. A friend invited me for a long ride from Ikebukuro to Mizuhodai. New city, new roads, but the same love for cycling.

Bangladesh Independence Ride — My First Medal

I joined the Bangladesh Independence 2.0 cycling event. Completing the 24 km route and earning my first medal made me proud. That moment pushed me to keep cycling even more.
